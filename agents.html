DUELISTS

Jett

Jett uses mobility and agility to outplay enemies.

  • Cloudburst: Small smoke.
  • Updraft: Jump upward.
  • Tailwind: Dash quickly.
  • Blade Storm: Throw knives.

Phoenix

Phoenix uses fire to control areas and heal himself.

  • Curveball: Curve flash.
  • Hot Hands: Fireball heal/damage.
  • Blaze: Fire wall.
  • Run It Back: Respawn after death.

Reyna

Self-sustaining duelist who feeds on enemy kills.

  • Leer: Blind enemies.
  • Devour: Heal on kill.
  • Dismiss: Become intangible.
  • Empress: Enhanced combat mode.

Raze

Neon
