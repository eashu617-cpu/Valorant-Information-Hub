Welcome to ValoHub


ValoHub is your go-to destination for everything related to Valorant — from understanding game modes to improving your gameplay.

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical first-person shooter where precise gunplay blends with unique agent abilities. Two teams, Attackers and Defenders, compete in strategic rounds where teamwork, communication, and skill decide victory.


How the game works

    Each match is played in rounds with clear objectives:

  • Attackers must plant the Spike at one of the bomb sites.
  • Defenders must stop the plant or defuse the Spike once it is planted.
  • A round can also be won by eliminating the entire enemy team.
  • Players earn credits every round to purchase weapons, shields, and abilities, making economy management an important part of the game.

    Game Modes


    Valorant offers multiple game modes to suit different playstyles:

  • Unrated – Standard full-length match with no rank impact
  • Competitive – Ranked mode where performance affects your rank
  • Swiftplay – Faster version of Unrated with shorter rounds
  • Spike Rush – Fun, short matches with random weapons and power-ups
  • Deathmatch – Free-for-all mode focused on aim practice
  • Team Deathmatch – 5v5 respawn-based combat mode
  • Escalation – Gun progression mode where weapons change after kills
  • Premier – Tournament-style mode for organized team competition
